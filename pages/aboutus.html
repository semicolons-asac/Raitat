About Us

RAITAT is a website specialized in rateing restaurants according to the services provided. Currently we have four main categories, pizza, shawarma, burger and Gulf food. It was established in January / 2021, by software development students. RAITAT provides you with many features such as displaying the highest rated restaurants in each category, evaluating any restaurant you tried in an easy and fun way, and you can also add your own list of reviews. We at RITAT welcome all restaurants to join our family, and to share this fun experience with visitors. We are working on adding other categories and many distinctive services to meet your desires. You can follow all of our news on the below communication sites. We are happy with any addition or suggestion from you, for more information please contact us by calling the customer service number directly or via WhatsApp.
